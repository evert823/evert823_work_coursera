Recap: The RSS and the Gradient in matrix form

\section{The formula for RSS}
\[
\mathrm{RSS(w)} = \left(y - H w\right)^{\top} \left(y - H w\right)
\]

\section{The formula for the Gradient}
\[
\nabla\, \mathrm{RSS(w)} = -2 H^{\top} \left(y - H w\right)
\]
